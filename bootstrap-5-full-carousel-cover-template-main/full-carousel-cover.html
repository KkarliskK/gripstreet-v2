    About GripStreet

    GripStreet is a car club in Cēsis, Latvia, uniting car enthusiasts who share a passion for driving, car culture, and community. We organize regular meetups, road trips where members can connect, learn, and explore Latvia’s scenic roads together. Whether you’re a seasoned car lover or new to the scene, GripStreet welcomes all who want to share in the thrill of the automotive world. Join us and be part of Latvia's most passionate car community!

    How can you join GripStreet?

    It is easy, just fill out the email form below this post and wait for a response. After you have made it in the club, there will be a month long security period, to show that you are active enough and not just a "chat-reader". After the test month you will earn the GripStreet sticker as a symbol that you are welcomed in the club and passed the test month.

    When is our next event?

    GripStreet, is trying to join all possible events as a car club. Events are not so often, but that is why everyone can just comunicate in the group chat to call out a cars & coffe meet at the local parking lot.

    Does GripsStreet make any events?

    No, GripStreet, does not create any open type events. The only events that the club creates, are the ones only for the club members, like late night cruising, cars & coffe and other type events.
